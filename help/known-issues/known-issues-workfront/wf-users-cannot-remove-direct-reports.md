# Users: Cannot remove direct reports 

When a user attempts to remove direct reports from a user, they cannot update the Direct Reports field if it already has a value. 

**Workaround**

Try one of the following:

* Add a new value, then remove the values.
* Remove the direct report from the user's Org Chart.

_First reported on January 28, 2026._
